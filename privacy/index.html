Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Perfit Video Generator ("the App") handles your information. The App is a desktop application that runs on your own computer. It does not operate a server or cloud backend of its own.

1. Summary

Everything the App uses to function is stored locally on your own computer. The App does not collect analytics, does not maintain a user database, and does not transmit your data to any service other than TikTok's own APIs (only when you explicitly ask the App to connect an account or publish a video).

2. Information the App handles

The App works with the following categories of data:

  • TikTok OAuth tokens. When you connect a TikTok account, the App receives and stores an access token and a refresh token. These are stored on your own computer in the App's config directory (~/.config/ai-video-editor-smart/tt_accounts/) with owner-only file permissions.
  • Your TikTok display name and avatar URL. Fetched via TikTok's user.info.basic scope after you connect. Used solely to label the connected account in the App's interface so you know which account will receive a published video. Stored locally.
  • Video files and captions that you create. Stored on your own computer. Sent to TikTok only when you explicitly click Post or Schedule.
  • App configuration. Your preferences, project files, schedule entries, and similar local state. Stored on your own computer.

3. Data sent to third parties

The App sends data to TikTok's APIs only when you explicitly take an action that requires it:

  • Connecting an account: the App uses TikTok's OAuth endpoints to exchange an authorization code for an access token and to fetch your display name and avatar URL.
  • Publishing a video: the App sends the video file, caption, privacy setting, and interaction-toggle preferences you chose to TikTok's Content Posting API endpoints.
  • Refreshing tokens: when an access token nears expiry, the App exchanges the refresh token for a new access token via TikTok's OAuth endpoint.

No other third parties receive any of your data. The App has no analytics, no telemetry, no cloud backup, and no remote error reporting.

4. Your control over your data

All data the App stores is on your own computer. You can:

  • Delete a connected TikTok account from the App's Accounts panel, which removes the stored tokens and profile info from your computer.
  • Revoke the App's TikTok access at any time via your TikTok account settings at tiktok.com/setting/apps-permissions.
  • Delete the App's config directory (~/.config/ai-video-editor-smart/) to remove all locally stored data.
  • Uninstall the App.

5. Children

The App is not directed at children under 13 and should not be used by them.

6. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent version.

7. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be directed to the developer via the project's GitHub issue tracker.
