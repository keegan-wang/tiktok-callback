Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to Mass Gen ("the App"), a desktop application developed by Keegan. Mass Gen runs on your own computer and helps you bulk-generate product videos and publish them to TikTok and Instagram. It does not operate a server or cloud backend of its own.

1. Summary

Everything Mass Gen uses to function is stored locally on your own computer. The App does not collect analytics, does not maintain a user database, and does not transmit your data to any service other than TikTok's and Instagram's own APIs — and only when you explicitly ask it to connect an account or publish a video.

2. Information the App handles

  • TikTok OAuth tokens. When you connect a TikTok account, Mass Gen receives and stores an access token and refresh token on your computer in the App's config directory with owner-only file permissions.
  • Instagram OAuth tokens. When you connect an Instagram account, Mass Gen receives and stores an access token on your computer, also with owner-only file permissions.
  • Your display name and avatar URL from TikTok/Instagram. Fetched once after connecting, stored locally, used only to label connected accounts in the App's interface.
  • Video files, product images, and captions that you create. Stored on your own computer. Sent to TikTok or Instagram only when you explicitly click Post or Schedule.
  • App configuration and project files. Preferences, schedule entries, and publishing history. Stored on your own computer.

3. Data sent to third parties

Mass Gen sends data to TikTok's or Instagram's APIs only when you take an action that requires it:

  • Connecting an account: OAuth exchange for access token and basic profile info.
  • Publishing a video: the video file and caption you chose are sent to the platform's Content Posting API.
  • Refreshing tokens: expired tokens are refreshed via the platform's OAuth endpoint.

No other third parties receive any of your data. Mass Gen has no analytics, no telemetry, no cloud backup, and no remote error reporting.

4. Your control over your data

  • Delete a connected account from the App's Accounts panel to remove tokens from your computer.
  • Revoke TikTok access at any time via tiktok.com/setting/apps-permissions.
  • Revoke Instagram access via your Instagram account's connected apps settings.
  • Delete the App's config directory to remove all locally stored data.
  • Uninstall the App.

5. Children

Mass Gen is not directed at children under 13 and should not be used by them.

6. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent version.
